PMC Education Management

An AI-assisted learning platform designed to transform students and professionals into Project Managers, Business Analysts, and Consultants through structured workflows.

View Live Product

Problem

Many learners struggle to transition into professional roles due to lack of structured guidance, real-world workflows, and consistent execution systems.

Solution

The PMC Smart Learning System introduces a structured approach combining AI-generated planning, task execution, and progress tracking to guide users toward career transformation.
